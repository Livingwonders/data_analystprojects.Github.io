NovaMed Solutions Sales Performance Analysis

This is an insight-driven project with dataset of customers across countries and continents especially America and Europe for better sales Performance. Key performance indicators show a robust profit margin over the past 4 years, with stable month-over-month growth. Also, customer concentration and seasonal dips in mid-year margins signal the need for retention strategies and counter-seasonal campaigns. Our recommendations to the company improves customer retention by 15% within 4 months and with possibility of improving to 35% within a year. The analysis was carried out using Excel & Power BI.

Suicides rate analysis using Power BI

A mental health organization contacted for insight into its datasets as its advocacy and efforts make little or no impact on suicidal rates across the countries it covered. The analysis unveiled that primarily age and gender has significant effect on suicidal rate with 33.89% from Male gender with age bracket 35 - 54 years old. Our recommendations will help to reduce suicidal rate by 15% within the first quarter of its application.

Customers & Products Analysis using Pivot chart, Excel

A named company was faced with the challenge of improving its overall operational efficiency and customer experience. My team was consulted to carry out to detailed analysis, identify key problems and provide effective recommendations. The company have its businesses in four locations and each of which is faced with unique problems including, poor inventory management (overstocking & understocking), high expenditure, poor performances by staff and poor customers satisfaction. Recommendation provided enhanced inventory management by 25% within 5 months of its implementation, this inturn improve customer retention by about 15% within the stated period.
